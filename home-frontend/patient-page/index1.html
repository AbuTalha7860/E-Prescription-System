Frequently Asked Questions

What is an online doctor consultation or online medical consultation?
An online doctor consultation or online medical consultation is a virtual visit with a doctor where patients can discuss their health concerns and receive medical advice and treatment plans without having to visit a clinic in person.
How do I consult a doctor online now?
To consult a doctor online, you need to visit a telemedicine platform, choose the type of specialist you need, book an appointment, and then connect with the doctor via video call, phone call, or chat at the scheduled time.
Do you provide online doctor consultation for emergencies?
Online doctor consultations are typically not suitable for emergencies. For urgent medical situations, it's best to visit an emergency room or call emergency services immediately.
During the online doctor consultation, the audio/video was not clear.
If the audio or video quality is poor during an online consultation, try checking your internet connection, switching to a different device, or moving to a location with better connectivity. You can also contact the support team of the telemedicine platform for assistance.
What is the minimum fee for online doctor consultation?
The minimum fee for an online doctor consultation can vary depending on the platform and the specialist. It typically ranges from $10 to $50 per consultation.
How do I pay for the online/offline doctor consultations?
Payments for online or offline doctor consultations can usually be made through various methods such as credit/debit cards, online banking, e-wallets, or other payment gateways provided by the telemedicine platform or clinic.
Will I get a refund if I cancel the online doctor consultation?
Refund policies vary by platform. Some platforms may offer a full or partial refund if you cancel the appointment within a certain time frame, while others may have a no-refund policy. Check the specific platform's cancellation and refund policy for details.
What is a specialist doctor?
A specialist doctor is a medical professional who has completed advanced education and training in a specific area of medicine, such as cardiology, dermatology, neurology, etc. They focus on diagnosing and treating specific conditions related to their specialty.
When should you consult a specialist doctor?
You should consult a specialist doctor when you have specific health concerns that require expert attention, such as chronic conditions, persistent symptoms, or when referred by a general physician for specialized care.
Why is it best to consult a specialist doctor?
Consulting a specialist doctor is beneficial because they have extensive knowledge and expertise in their field, allowing them to provide accurate diagnoses, effective treatments, and comprehensive care for specific health issues.
